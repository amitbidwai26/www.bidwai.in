Lansdowne Pub

This application was developed on Android and iOS platforms for Lansdowne Pub located in Boston, U.S.A. It gives users an insight of the deals of the pub and is loaded with multiple utility options like booking a cab, booking a party, accessing Pub TV and connecting to the pub's social media channels. A simple push of a button will deliver you notifications on the latest hot deals on food and liquor.

Features

  • Receive location-based alert notifications on pub's deals.
  • Users can easily register using social media accounts.
  • Updates on music band schedules, and direct access to their official websites and music videos.
  • Access to Pub Menu including drinks, food, daily and weekly specials.
  • Augmented reality on pub logo scanning.

Health Above 60

Health Above 60, an iOS app engineered to turn an innovative idea of delivering on demand medical help for aged citizens at the comfort of their home into reality. This application features options that allow users to call medical professionals like doctors, nurses, physicians, physiotherapists, consultants and counselors with just a click and get the necessary medical aid as and when required.

Features

  • One-touch access to medical professionals.
  • On-demand availability of medical professionals right from doctors to attenders.
  • Easy-to-fill contact form for sending medical request.
  • Post reviews and feedback comments on the medical treatment

Health Opinion

Health Opinion, an Android application developed to better and speed up the access of people requiring medical advice towards medical experts. This app reduces the efforts, confusions and time involved in getting the right medical advices for simple and complex medical ailments. User queries are directly connected to medical experts who in return provided the necessary treatment and advise required for the patient.

Features

  • Health advice from surgeons and general physicians.
  • Social media integration to stay connected via social accounts.
  • Enquiry form with provisions to fill in health and contact details.
  • Account information to manage all feedbacks and comments.

Mello Digital

Mello Digital is a broadcasting app developed for Cornwall Broadcasting, the only Jamaican company that delivers 24/7 television live streaming videos. The app was incorporated with technologies that help in delivering uninterrupted live streaming and FM broadcasting and controls that lets users schedule programs and receive notifications. The app delivers regional, national and international happenings on a diverse fields and acts as a single interface for people to access news information.

Features

  • Notifications and alerts on events and shows.
  • Video on demand and program scheduling.
  • Access to Mello TV - Live streaming / telecasting.
  • Access to Mello FM - Live radio.
  • HTML 5 support for uninterrupted mobile video viewing.

Trim & Tone

Trim and Tone is an Android app with easy user interface used as a MP3 cutter and joiner application. Also, this app allows you to set the cut or joint part of resulting music on your smart phones as ringtone, notification, or alarm tone. Even it provides the option of recording too!

Features

  • Easy user interface.
  • Converts file to waveform format with 5 zoom levels.
  • Comes with indicator cursor and auto scrollable waveform.
